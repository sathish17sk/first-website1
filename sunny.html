    Synopsis

    Sunny Leone's visit to Kochi in 2017 for a smartphone brand launch created a frenzy among fans, reminiscent of Shah Rukh Khan's visit earlier. Thousands gathered at MG Road, causing a standstill, to catch a glimpse of the star. Sunny, touched by the love, expressed gratitude and admiration for Kerala, where she continues to enjoy a special bond with fans despite occasional criticism.

    Sunny Leone, known for her magnetic presence, caused a stir in Kochi back in 2017, marking a memorable moment in her career. As the actress celebrates her 43rd birthday today, let's take a throwback journey to that sunny day in Kochi when the city came to standtill just to grab a quick glance of their favourite star.

    Leone's popularity transcends borders, captivating not just the North but also the hearts of fans in Kerala and Tamil Nadu. Her hit song 'Baby Doll' from the movie 'Ragini MMS 2', released in 2014, became an instant hit down South, despite the language barrier.

    The Sunny Storm In Kochi: A Fan Frenzy

    In 2017, Sunny made headlines with her first visit to Kochi, leaving a lasting mark on the city. The occasion? The launch of a smartphone brand at MG Road, Kochi's bustling hub. The frenzy that ensued was nothing short of a Sunny storm, reminiscent of Shah Rukh Khan's visit in 2011 that caused a massive traffic block.

    Thousands of fans thronged MG Road, bringing the area to a standstill as they eagerly awaited a glimpse of their favorite star. Thankfully, Sunny was able to make her way to the dais with the help of police, to address the gathering.

    Sunny Expresses Gratitude and Love For Kerala

    Later, Sunny, overwhelmed by the love and support, expressed her gratitude in a tweet, saying, "No words...Can't thank the people of Kochi. Was so overwhelmed by the love & support. Never will forget God's Own Country Kerala!" She also shared a videos from her Kochi visit.

    — SunnyLeone (@SunnyLeone)

    The incident also highlighted the adoration Sunny receives from her fans. One memorable moment captured was when a young enthusiast tore a banner to capture a video of Sunny, a gesture that caught her attention and admiration.

    — SunnyLeone (@SunnyLeone)

    Since that eventful day, Sunny has maintained a special connection with Kerala. She revisited the state for various engagements, including shooting MTV Splitsvilla 13 and enjoying leisure time with her family in Thiruvananthapuram.

    Despite the event causing traffic disruptions and drawing criticism from some quarters, Sunny's visit remains a cherished memory for her fans and for her, a testament to the overwhelming love she receives wherever she goes.
